About Us

The padel club designed for you.

Welcome to DUS, where every detail is designed to deliver the ultimate padel experience. From premium courts and seamless booking to exceptional service and luxurious amenities, we create an environment for you to stay healthy, unwind, and connect—both on and off the court.

Whether you’re new to the sport or you’re a seasoned player, DUS offers padel for everyone. Play with friends, train with experts, or join group clinics to sharpen your skills and compete in skill-based tournaments.

Wherever you are in your journey, we have the padel for you.
